County Freight Profile

This data profile provides an introductory overview of freight activity in County. The movement of freight is an ever-growing feature of the local and county landscape that directly ties into economic development, environmental, land use, and transportation concerns. The data provided in this PhillyFreightFinder County Profile was sourced from various national, regional and local datasets and through studies conducted by the Delaware Valley Regional Planning Commission.
